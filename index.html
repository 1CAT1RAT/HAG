STINKY LOSERS

A small multiverse experiment - meet the characters, inspect the relationship matrix, and read the story threads that bind them.

Tip: click a character to view details. In the matrix view you can click a column header to see that character's total score (self-score excluded).

About

This project is a lightweight interactive prototype - built with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. Feel free to fork the repo and contribute.
